<decisions_made>

- **q single-fragment fallback**: Use `Object.values(auxMap)` instead of looping `auxMap[1..fragCount]`. The count-based loop failed silently when `formulaFragmentCounts` returned a count that didn't match the actual auxMap keys. `Object.values(auxMap)` is always correct for single-fragment since all atoms are in the one fragment.
- **q multi-fragment**: Keep the formula-range approach (`auxMap[cumulativeOffset+1..fragCount]`). This is necessary to exclude atoms from uncharged fragments — e.g. sulfate O- atoms must not be highlighted when the /q entry is only on the Cu fragment.
- **q formal-charge preference**: Within a charged fragment, prefer atoms with `atom.charge !== 0` in the Ketcher struct. This highlights the specific charged atom (quaternary N) rather than the whole fragment. Falls back to all fragment atoms for delocalized charge.
- **p mobile-H primary source**: Use mobile-H sites parsed from the /h layer (`parseMobileHydrogens`) instead of all heteroatoms. Fallback to all heteroatoms when no /h layer or no mobile-H sites exist.
</decisions_made>
